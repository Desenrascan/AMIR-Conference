Eurasian risk 2019

Events taken place as in Azerbaijan as all over the world feature intensification of global problems related to providing complex security of population and territories from natural and technological emergencies. We reveal all these facts in threats involving climate change, growth of number and difficulty of natural and technological emergencies. Undoubtedly it takes continuous study and monitoring risk sources for population and territories and search of effective mechanisms of minimizing their consequences and controlling them as well.

We are pleased to tell that some Eurasian countries have accumulated a considerable work experience on given problem. Nevertheless the world scientific society manifest anxiety that people and countries are victims of accidents and disasters, die and suffer, historical and material values are destroyed. We think that the reasons of situation like this are unsufficient explanatory work with people, estrangement them from the problem to warn and eliminate emergencies and also low-effective work on development of risk minimization system of natural and anthropogenic emergencies.

Emergencies remain one of the most serious obstacles on achieving sustainable development and providing security of countries. Therefore to solve the problem of minimizing disaster hazards through the analysis of risks and innovative control of them in order to create possibilities for providing and strengthening the sustainable development of countries, reliable protection of population from emergencies is one of the most important social and state problems. With regard to it we address to all scientists and specialists of Eurasian countries to intensify innovative solution of minimizing natural and technological hazards. Taking into consideration the presence of organic interrelation between the minimization of emergency hazards and sustainable development of the country we suppose that it is important to involve all interested partners in work on analyzing, estimating risks and managing them. By this form of address we call for all interested partners to take an active part in conference, exchange experience and knowledge, and make the contribution to common cause of continent steady progress.

Main aims of conference: to unite scientists and specialists from different fields of knowledge and grant them possibilities of exchanging information, ideas and innovative solutions of problem minimization within the natural and technological risks on Eurasian continent;

to promote knowledge transfer on risks, innovations and advanced technologies of risk minimization;

to assist understanding and professional interaction of scientists and organizations to make joint contribution to the theory and practice of risk analysis and innovative problem solution including the sphere of population and territory protection from natural, technological and anthropogenic emergencies;

to support Millennium Challenges and Sendai (Japan) Framework for Disaster Risk Reduction 2015-2030 under UNO as a basic document of strategical aims and priority trends and expected results as well;

to reveal innovations in identifying various kinds of natural and anthropogenic hazards, their analysis and assessment, indicating ways and methods of such administrative solutions to be taken which can provide population dwelling and steady progress of social and economic spheres of Eurasian continent different regions.

The AMIR Technical Services Company invites researchers, academics, practitioners and policy makers from the Eurasian region and beyond to discuss common issues of interest. Members of the appropriate International and other regional risk issued organizations are also most welcome to participate. We are sure that “EURASIAN RISK 2019” Conference will contribute to innovative solutions of important problems within analysis, assessment and minimization of natural and technological risks.
